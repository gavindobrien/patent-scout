A collapsible bicycle pedal assembly for commuter bikes. The pedal body
folds flat against the crank arm using a spring-loaded ratchet hinge that
locks automatically at full deployment (90 degrees from the crank arm) and
requires a deliberate twist-and-release motion to fold, preventing
accidental collapse under normal pedaling load. The hinge pin incorporates
a captive detent ball that seats into a machined groove at the locked
position, giving tactile and audible confirmation of full lock. The pedal
body itself uses a two-piece aluminum shell with an internal steel
reinforcement plate positioned only under the ball of the foot contact
zone, reducing weight relative to a solid pedal while maintaining load
rating under standard pedaling forces.
